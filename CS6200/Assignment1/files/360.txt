<p><b>Zapatero</b>  is a Spanish-language <a href="/wiki/Occupational_surname" class="mw-redirect" title="Occupational surname">occupational surname</a> literally meaning "<a href="/wiki/Shoemaking" title="Shoemaking">shoemaker</a>".
</p>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Jos%C3%A9_Luis_Rodr%C3%ADguez_Zapatero" title="José Luis Rodríguez Zapatero">José Luis Rodríguez Zapatero</a> (born 1960), Prime Minister of Spain from 2004 to 2011.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Carlos_Arroyo_(architect)" title="Carlos Arroyo (architect)">Carlos Arroyo Zapatero</a> (born 1964), Spanish architect.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Jos%C3%A9_Ignacio_Zapatero" title="José Ignacio Zapatero">José Ignacio Zapatero</a> (born 1971), Spanish rugby union player.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Isma_Pi%C3%B1era" title="Isma Piñera">Ismael Piñera Zapatero</a> (born 1971), Spanish soccer player.</li></ul>
