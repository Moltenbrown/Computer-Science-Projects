	<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Farmer (surname)</h1>
	
	<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
		<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>
		
		
		
		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
		<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Farmer</b> is an English surname. Although an occupationally derived surname, it was not given to tillers of the soil, but to collectors of taxes and tithes specializing in the collection of funds from farming leases.<sup id="cite_ref-surnamedb_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-surnamedb-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>  In 2000, there were 68,309 people with the last name Farmer in the United States, making it the 431st most common last name in the nation.<sup id="cite_ref-census_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-census-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
