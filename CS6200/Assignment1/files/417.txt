<p><b>Cidade da Maia</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Freguesia_(Portugal)" class="mw-redirect" title="Freguesia (Portugal)">civil parish</a> in the municipality of <a href="/wiki/Maia_Municipality" class="mw-redirect" title="Maia Municipality">Maia</a>, Portugal. It was formed in 2013 by the merger of the former parishes Gueifães, Maia and Vermoim.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The population in 2011 was 40,134,<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> in an area of 10.42&#160;km².<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The parish covers the central area of Maia. In 1902, Maia achieved the status of <i>vila</i> (town). Maia was granted city status (<i>cidade</i>) on 23 August 1986.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Sports">Sports</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Cidade_da_Maia&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Sports">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
