		<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Watson</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Patronymic_surname" title="Patronymic surname">patronymic surname</a> of <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> and Scottish origin. Meaning "son of Walter", the popular <a href="/wiki/Old_English" title="Old English">Old English</a> given names "Wat" or "Watt" were diminutive forms of the name Walter. Watson is 46th-most common surname in England and nineteenth most common in Scotland.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
