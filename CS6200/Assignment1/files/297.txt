<p><b>Hill</b> is a surname of <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> origin, meaning "a person who lived on a hill", or derived from the Greek or Latin name <a href="/wiki/Hilary_(name)" title="Hilary (name)">Hilary</a> or Hillary. It is the 36th most common surname in England and 37th most common in the United States.<sup id="cite_ref-fb_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
