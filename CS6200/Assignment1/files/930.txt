<p><b>Johansen</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Scandinavia" title="Scandinavia">Scandinavian</a> <a href="/wiki/Patronymic" title="Patronymic">patronymic</a> <a href="/wiki/Surname" title="Surname">surname</a> meaning <i>"son of <a href="/wiki/Johan_(given_name)" title="Johan (given name)">Johan</a>"</i>. It is most common in <a href="/wiki/Denmark" title="Denmark">Denmark</a> and <a href="/wiki/Norway" title="Norway">Norway</a>.  The <a href="/wiki/Sweden" title="Sweden">Swedish</a> variant is <a href="/wiki/Johansson" title="Johansson">Johansson</a>, while the most common spelling in the US is <a href="/wiki/Johanson" title="Johanson">Johanson</a>.  There are still other spellings.  Johansen is an uncommon <a href="/wiki/Given_name" title="Given name">given name</a>.  People with the surname Johansen include:
