	<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Walker (surname)</h1>
	
	<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
		<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>
		
		
		
		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
		<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><table class="infobox" style="width:22em"><caption>Walker</caption><tbody><tr><th scope="row">Pronunciation</th><td><span class="nowrap"><span class="IPA nopopups noexcerpt"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/English" title="Help:IPA/English">/<span style="border-bottom:1px dotted"><span title="/ˈ/: primary stress follows">ˈ</span><span title="&#39;w&#39; in &#39;wind&#39;">w</span><span title="/ɔː/: &#39;au&#39; in &#39;fraud&#39;">ɔː</span><span title="&#39;k&#39; in &#39;kind&#39;">k</span><span title="/ər/: &#39;er&#39; in &#39;letter&#39;">ər</span></span>/</a></span></span></td></tr><tr><th scope="row"><a href="/wiki/Language" title="Language">Language</a>(s)</th><td><a href="/wiki/English_language" title="English language">English</a>, <a href="/wiki/Scots_language" title="Scots language">Scots</a><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>, <a href="/wiki/Middle_High_German" title="Middle High German">Middle High German</a>, <a href="/wiki/German_language" title="German language">German</a></td></tr><tr><th colspan="2" style="text-align:center;background:#ADD8E6">Origin</th></tr><tr><th scope="row">Meaning</th><td>A <i><a href="/wiki/Fulling" title="Fulling">walker</a>,</i> or one who "walked" on raw, damp cloth in order to thicken it.</td></tr><tr><th scope="row">Region of origin</th><td>Europe</td></tr><tr><th colspan="2" style="text-align:center;background:#ADD8E6">Other names</th></tr><tr><th scope="row">Variant form(s)</th><td><a href="/wiki/Welker" title="Welker">Welker</a>, Walcher, Welcker, Wallker, Walkyr, Walkere, <i>Wealcan</i>, <i>Walcere</i></td></tr></tbody></table>
<p><b>Walker</b> is an <a href="/wiki/English_name" title="English name">English</a> and <a href="/wiki/German_name" title="German name">German</a> <a href="/wiki/Surname" title="Surname">surname</a> derived from either a <a href="/wiki/Fulling" title="Fulling">fuller</a>, from the <a href="/wiki/Middle_High_German" title="Middle High German">Middle High German</a> <i>walker,</i><sup id="cite_ref-:0_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> meaning "a fuller of cloth", or an officer whose duty consisted of walking or inspecting a certain part of a forest. The surname "Walker" was first found in Germany where records of the name date back to the thirteenth century. In 1253, Sigelo dictus Welkere was recorded in <a href="/wiki/Wei%C3%9Fenburg_in_Bayern" title="Weißenburg in Bayern">Weissenburg</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-:0_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and in 1209, Bruno Welkiner was a citizen of <a href="/wiki/Cologne" title="Cologne">Cologne</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The Walker Families of <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a> are believed to originate from <a href="/wiki/Yorkshire" title="Yorkshire">Yorkshire</a><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>.
</p><p>With close to 100,000 bearers, Walker is the 18th most common surname in England.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> As of the 2000 U.S. Census, 501,307 people had the surname Walker, making it the 28th most common surname in America.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> It is the 14th most common surname in Australia, with 26,688 people as of 2007.<sup id="cite_ref-Australia1_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Australia1-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
