It is a <a href="/wiki/Patronymic" title="Patronymic">patronymic</a> form of the <a href="/wiki/Given_name" title="Given name">given name</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Nichol" title="Nichol">Nichol</a></i>, which was a common medieval form of <i><a href="/wiki/Nicholas" title="Nicholas">Nicholas</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-lastn_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lastn-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
