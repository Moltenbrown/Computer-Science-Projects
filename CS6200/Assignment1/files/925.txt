<p><b>Giovanni</b> is a male Italian <a href="/wiki/Given_name" title="Given name">given name</a> (from <a href="/wiki/Latin" title="Latin">Latin</a> <i>Ioannes</i>). It is the Italian equivalent of <a href="/wiki/John_(name)" class="mw-redirect" title="John (name)">John</a>. Giovanni is frequently contracted to <a href="/wiki/Gianni" title="Gianni">Gianni</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gian" title="Gian">Gian</a>, or <a href="/wiki/Gio_(nickname)" title="Gio (nickname)">Gio</a>, particularly in the name <a href="/wiki/Gianbattista" class="mw-redirect" title="Gianbattista">Gianbattista</a>, and can also be found as a surname. It is sometimes spelled as Geovanni, Giovonnie or Giovannie when used as an English female name. Its female counterpart is <a href="/wiki/Giovanna" title="Giovanna">Giovanna</a>.
</p>
