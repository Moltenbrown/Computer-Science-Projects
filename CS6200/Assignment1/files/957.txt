<p><b>Stephenson</b> is a medieval <a href="/wiki/Patronymic_surname" title="Patronymic surname">patronymic surname</a> meaning "son of <a href="/wiki/Stephen" title="Stephen">Stephen</a>". The earliest public record is found in the county of <a href="/wiki/Huntingdonshire" title="Huntingdonshire">Huntingdonshire</a> in 1279. There are variant spellings including <a href="/wiki/Stevenson" title="Stevenson">Stevenson</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> People with the surname include:
