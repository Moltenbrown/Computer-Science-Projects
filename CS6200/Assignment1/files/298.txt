	<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Bush (surname)</h1>
	
	<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">

		<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The surname <b>Bush</b> is an <a href="/wiki/English_language" title="English language">English</a> surname, derived from either the <a href="/wiki/Old_English" title="Old English">Old English</a> word "busc" or the <a href="/wiki/Old_Norse" title="Old Norse">Old Norse</a> "buskr," both of which mean "bush," a shrub.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>  
</p><p>Variations on the English spelling "Bush" include: Bushe, Bosch, Boush, Boushe, <a href="/wiki/Busch_(disambiguation)" class="mw-redirect mw-disambig" title="Busch (disambiguation)">Busch</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bussche_family" title="Bussche family">Bussche</a>, Buscher, Bysh, and <a href="/w/index.php?title=Bysshe&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Bysshe (page does not exist)">Bysshe</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The Bush family has held a <a href="/wiki/Family_seat" title="Family seat">family seat</a> in <a href="/wiki/Yorkshire" title="Yorkshire">Yorkshire</a>, Northern England.
