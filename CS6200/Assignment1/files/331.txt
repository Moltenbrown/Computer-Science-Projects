<p><b>Luis Telmo Paz y Miño Estrella</b> (15 April 1884&#8211;1962), more commonly known as <b>Telmo Paz y Miño</b>, was President of the <a href="/wiki/Military_junta" title="Military junta">Supreme Military Junta</a> of <a href="/wiki/Ecuador" title="Ecuador">Ecuador</a> in July 1925.
</p><p>Born in the then-<a href="/wiki/Rural" class="mw-redirect" title="Rural">rural</a> <a href="/wiki/Parroquia_(Ecuador)" class="mw-redirect" title="Parroquia (Ecuador)">parroquia</a> of <a href="/wiki/Chillogallo" title="Chillogallo">Chillogallo</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Quito" title="Quito">Quito</a>, Ecuador, Gen. Paz y Miño entered military school in 1902.
</p><p>He held various military and political positions, as well as having been rector of an educational institute. He also wrote various investigative works in geography, history and literature.
</p>
