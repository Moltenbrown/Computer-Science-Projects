<p><b>Hassan</b> (also spelled <b>Hasan</b>, <b>Hassane</b>, <b>Hassaan</b>, <b>Hassen</b>, <b>Hasson</b>, <b>Hassin</b>, <b>Hassine</b>, <b>Hacen</b>, <b>Hasen</b>, <b>Hasin</b>, <b>Hass</b>, <b>Hassa</b>, <b>Hasa</b>, <b>Hess</b>, <b>Cassin</b>, <b>Chassan</b>, <b>Chasan</b>, <b>Chasson</b>, <b>Chason</b>, <b>Khassan</b>, <b>Khasan</b>, <b>Cassan</b>, <b>Casan</b>, <b>Hazan</b>, <b>Hasso</b>, <b>Hassanein</b>, <b>Hasnen</b>, <b>Hassani</b>, <b>Hasani</b>, <b>Alhassan</b>, <b>Al-Hassan</b>, <b>Lassana</b>, <b>Alassane</b>, <b>Lacen</b>, <b>Lasanah</b>, <b>Assan</b>, <b>Asan</b>, <b>Asanov/Asanova</b>, <b>Hasanov/Hasanova</b>, <b>Khasanov/Khasanova</b>, <b>Hasanoff</b>, <b>Jasanoff</b>, <b>Hasanović</b>, <b>Hasanovic</b>, <b>Asanović</b>, <b>Hasanovich</b>, <b>Hasanovski/Hasanovska</b>, <b>Asanovski/Asanovska</b>, <b>O'Hassan</b>, <b>Haasan</b>, or <b>Hasaan</b>) is an <a href="/wiki/Arabic" title="Arabic">Arabic</a>, <a href="/wiki/Irish_people" title="Irish people">Irish</a>, or <a href="/wiki/Scottish_people" title="Scottish people">Scottish</a> surname.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology_and_spelling">Etymology and spelling</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hassan_(surname)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Etymology and spelling">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>There are several unrelated origins for this surname:
</p>
<ul><li>In <a href="/wiki/Arabic" title="Arabic">Arabic</a>, Hassan is a transliteration of two names that both derive from the <a href="/wiki/Triconsonantal" class="mw-redirect" title="Triconsonantal">triconsonantal</a> root <a href="/wiki/%E1%B8%A4-S-N" title="Ḥ-S-N">Ḥ-S-N</a>: <i><i lang="ar-Latn" title="Arabic-language romanization">Ḥasan</i></i> (<span lang="ar" dir="rtl" title="Arabic language text">حسن</span>), which means "handsome", "manly", "strong", or "good", and <i><i lang="ar-Latn" title="Arabic-language romanization">Ḥassān</i></i> (&#8207;<span lang="ar" dir="rtl"><a href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%D8%AD%D8%B3%D8%A7%D9%86#Arabic" class="extiw" title="wikt:حسان">حسان</a></span>&#8206;), which means "benefactor".</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>/<a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>, the surname Hassan is one of the anglicized forms of the <a href="/wiki/Gaelic_language" class="mw-redirect" title="Gaelic language">Gaelic</a> (<a href="/wiki/Irish_language" title="Irish language">Irish</a>/<a href="/wiki/Scottish_Gaelic" title="Scottish Gaelic">Scottish</a>) form of Ó hOsáin.  It is to be distinguished from Ó hOisín and Ó hOiseáin (<a href="/wiki/Hession_(surname)" class="mw-redirect" title="Hession (surname)">Hession</a> and <a href="/w/index.php?title=Hishon&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Hishon (page does not exist)">Hishon</a>).  In <a href="/wiki/County_Londonderry" title="County Londonderry">County Londonderry</a>, where it is numerous, it is spelt Hassan, Hassen, Hasson, Hassin and Hessin.  In the Monaghan Hearth Money Rolls of 1663, it appears as O'Hassan.  There was a Hasson of Wexford among the "principal gentlemen" of that county in 1598, but that family was no doubt of non-Gaelic stock, and a John Hassan was an influential merchant in Wexford fifty years earlier.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Another derivation is from "Hal's son".<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
<dl><dt>Depending on language and region, spelling variations</dt></dl>
<p>Hassan is a surname of many independent linguistic and regional origins, including <a href="/wiki/Arabic" title="Arabic">Arabic</a> (and through the influence of Arabic, languages spoken by <a href="/wiki/Muslim" class="mw-redirect" title="Muslim">Muslims</a> such as <a href="/wiki/Persian_language" title="Persian language">Persian</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kurdish_languages" title="Kurdish languages">Kurdish</a>, <a href="/wiki/Urdu" title="Urdu">Urdu</a>, <a href="/wiki/Indonesian_language" title="Indonesian language">Indonesian</a>, <a href="/wiki/Malaysian_language" title="Malaysian language">Malaysian</a>, <a href="/wiki/Turkish_language" title="Turkish language">Turkish</a>, <a href="/wiki/Uyghur_Khaganate" title="Uyghur Khaganate">Uyghur</a>, <a href="/wiki/Turkmen_language" title="Turkmen language">Turkmen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Somali_language" title="Somali language">Somali</a>, <a href="/wiki/Swahili_language" title="Swahili language">Swahili</a>, <a href="/wiki/Berber_language" class="mw-redirect" title="Berber language">Berber</a>, <a href="/wiki/Azerbaijani_language" title="Azerbaijani language">Azerbaijani</a>, <a href="/wiki/Crimean_Tatar_language" title="Crimean Tatar language">Crimean Tatar</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tatar_language" title="Tatar language">Tatar</a>,  <a href="/wiki/Bosnian_language" title="Bosnian language">Bosnian</a>, <a href="/wiki/Albanian_language" title="Albanian language">Albanian</a>, etc.), as well as <a href="/wiki/Irish_language" title="Irish language">Irish</a> and <a href="/wiki/Scottish_Gaelic" title="Scottish Gaelic">Scottish</a>.
</p><p>Therefore, depending on language and region, spelling variations include Hasan, Hassen, Hasson, Hassaan, Hassin, Hassine, Hacen, Hasen, Hasin, Hass, Hassa, Hasa, Hess, Cassin, Chassan, Chasan, Chasson, Chason, Khassan, Khasan, Cassan, Casan, Hasso, Hassanein, Hasnen, Hassani, Hasani, Alhassan, Al-Hassan, Lassana, Alassane, Lacen, Lasanah, Assan, Asan, Asanov/Asanova, Hasanov/Hasanova, Khasanov/Khasanova, Hasanoff, Jasanoff, Hasanović, Hasanovic, Asanović, Hasanovich, Hasanovski/Hasanovska, Asanovski/Asanovska, O'Hassan, Haasan, or Hasaan.
</p>
<dl><dt>List of variant spellings</dt></dl>
<ul><li>In <a href="/wiki/Arabic_language" class="mw-redirect" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a> transcription: <span lang="ar" dir="rtl" title="Arabic language text">حسان ,حسن</span> (<i><i lang="ar-Latn" title="Arabic-language romanization">Ḥasan</i></i>)</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/Turkish_language" title="Turkish language">Turkish</a>: Hasan, Asan, Hasanoglu, Asanoglu</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/Bosnian_language" title="Bosnian language">Bosnian</a>: Hasanović</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/Albanian_language" title="Albanian language">Albanian</a>: Hasani or Hasan</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/Armenian_language" title="Armenian language">Armenian</a>: Hassanian or Hasanian</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/Azerbaijanian_language" class="mw-redirect" title="Azerbaijanian language">Azerbaijan</a>: Həsən</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/Kurdish_language" class="mw-redirect" title="Kurdish language">Kurdish</a>: Hesan</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/Kazakh_language" title="Kazakh language">Kazakh</a>: Asan or Äsem</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/Somali_language" title="Somali language">Somali</a>: Xasan</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/West_Africa" title="West Africa">West Africa</a>: <a href="/wiki/Lassana" title="Lassana">Lassana</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alassane" title="Alassane">Alassane</a> and Lacen, derived from <i>al-Hassan</i>.</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/French_language" title="French language">French</a>: Hassan, Hassen, Hasson or Hacen</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/Spanish_language" title="Spanish language">Spanish</a>: Hassan, Hassán, Hacen, Hacén, Jassan, Jassán, Jasan, Jasán, Hasson or Jasanoff</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/Italian_language" title="Italian language">Italian</a>: Cassan, Cassano, Cassani, Hasson, Gazzano, Gazzani, Gassano, Gassani.</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/Bulgarian_language" title="Bulgarian language">Bulgarian</a> (in <a href="/wiki/Cyrillic" class="mw-redirect" title="Cyrillic">Cyrillic</a>): Хасан, Асанов/Хасанов (masculine), Асанова/Хасанова (feminine)</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a> (in Cyrillic): Хасан, Хассан, Хассен, Хэссан, Гасан, Асан, Асанов/Хасанов (masculine), Асанова/Хасанова (feminine)</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/Macedonian_language" title="Macedonian language">Macedonian</a> (in Cyrillic): Хасан, Хасановски/Асановски (masculine), Хасановска/Асановска (feminine)</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/Croatian_language" title="Croatian language">Croatian</a>/<a href="/wiki/Serbian_language" title="Serbian language">Serbian</a>/<a href="/wiki/Montenegrin_language" title="Montenegrin language">Montenegrin</a>: Asanović or Hasanović</li>
<li>In <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>, some Muslims believe that <a href="/wiki/Hui_people#Surnames" title="Hui people">their surname</a> Ha (<span lang="zh" title="Chinese language text"><a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E5%93%88%E5%A7%93" class="extiw" title="zh:哈姓">哈</a></span>) is abbreviated from Arabic <i>Hassan</i>.</li></ul>
<dl><dt>Popularity of the surname</dt></dl>
<ul><li>The popularity of the name Hassan or its variants Hasson, Hassen, Hassin is not only in the <a href="/wiki/Arab_world" title="Arab world">Arab world</a> (including <a href="/wiki/Arab_Christian" class="mw-redirect" title="Arab Christian">Arab Christians</a>) but also in the <a href="/wiki/Muslim_world" title="Muslim world">Muslim world</a>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Irish</a> last name Hassan or its variants Hasson, Hassen, Hassin is frequently found especially in the area of <a href="/wiki/Derry" title="Derry">Derry</a> in <a href="/wiki/Northern_Ireland" title="Northern Ireland">Northern Ireland</a> and also everywhere where there is a sizable <a href="/wiki/Irish_diaspora" title="Irish diaspora">Irish diaspora</a> like in <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a>, <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a> and <a href="/wiki/New_Zealand" title="New Zealand">New Zealand</a>.</li>
<li>The frequency of the last name Hassan among Jews is mostly among <a href="/wiki/Sephardic_Jews" class="mw-redirect" title="Sephardic Jews">Sephardic</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mizrahi_Jews" title="Mizrahi Jews">Mizrahi Jews</a>.</li></ul>
