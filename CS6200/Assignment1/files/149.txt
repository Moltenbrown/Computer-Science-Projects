(name)">Hans</a></td></tr></tbody></table><p><b>Johan</b> is a masculine <a href="/wiki/Given_name" title="Given name">given name</a> of <a href="/wiki/Hebrew" class="mw-redirect" title="Hebrew">Hebrew</a> origin. It is a shortened form of the Hebrew name יְהוֹחָנָן (Yəhôḥānān), meaning "God is gracious", and uncommon as a <a href="/wiki/Surname" title="Surname">surname</a>. <i>Johan</i> is also a masculine given name of <a href="/wiki/Malay_language" title="Malay language">Malay language</a> origin, meaning "<a href="/wiki/Champion" title="Champion">Champion</a>".
