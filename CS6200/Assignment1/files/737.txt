	<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Eastern Mediterranean</h1>
	
	<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
		<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>
		
		
		
		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
		<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:152px;"><a href="/wiki/File:East_Mediterranean_sea.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/53/East_Mediterranean_sea.jpg" decoding="async" width="150" height="216" class="thumbimage" data-file-width="150" data-file-height="216" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:East_Mediterranean_sea.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>The Eastern Mediterranean</div></div></div>
<p>The <b>Eastern Mediterranean</b> denotes the countries geographically to the east of the <a href="/wiki/Mediterranean_Sea" title="Mediterranean Sea">Mediterranean Sea</a> (the <a href="/wiki/Levantine_Sea" title="Levantine Sea">Levantine Sea</a>). Its populations share not only geographic position but also cuisine, certain customs and a long, intertwined history.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Regions">Regions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Eastern_Mediterranean&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Regions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:252px;"><a href="/wiki/File:A-3-37-69-East-Meditteranean.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/76/A-3-37-69-East-Meditteranean.jpg/250px-A-3-37-69-East-Meditteranean.jpg" decoding="async" width="250" height="183" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/76/A-3-37-69-East-Meditteranean.jpg/375px-A-3-37-69-East-Meditteranean.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/76/A-3-37-69-East-Meditteranean.jpg/500px-A-3-37-69-East-Meditteranean.jpg 2x" data-file-width="3000" data-file-height="2200" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:A-3-37-69-East-Meditteranean.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Nicolas_Sanson" title="Nicolas Sanson">Nicolas Sanson</a>. Map of Eastern Mediterranean, <a href="/wiki/1651" title="1651">1651</a></div></div></div>
<p>This eastern Mediterranean region is commonly interpreted in two ways:
</p>
<ul><li>The more broad definition of the Levant which includes its historically tied neighboring countries, Greece and Egypt.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Syria_(region)" title="Syria (region)">region of Syria</a> with the island of <a href="/wiki/Cyprus" title="Cyprus">Cyprus</a> (also known as the <a href="/wiki/Levant" title="Levant">Levant</a>), and <a href="/wiki/Turkey" title="Turkey">Turkey</a>, which limits the definition to <a href="/wiki/Western_Asia" title="Western Asia">Western Asia</a></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Countries">Countries</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Eastern_Mediterranean&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Countries">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The countries and territories of the Eastern Mediterranean include <a href="/wiki/Cyprus" title="Cyprus">Cyprus</a>, <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greece</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lebanon" title="Lebanon">Lebanon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Syria" title="Syria">Syria</a>, <a href="/wiki/Israel" title="Israel">Israel</a>, <a href="/wiki/State_of_Palestine" title="State of Palestine">Palestine</a>, <a href="/wiki/Turkey" title="Turkey">Turkey</a>, <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Libya" title="Libya">Libya</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Jordan" title="Jordan">Jordan</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Eastern_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Eastern-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Cyprus_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Cyprus-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Greece_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Greece-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
<sup id="cite_ref-Cyprus_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Cyprus-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> The countries under the term <i>North-eastern Mediterranean</i> could sometimes include <a href="/wiki/Albania" title="Albania">Albania</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bosnia_and_Herzegovina" title="Bosnia and Herzegovina">Bosnia and Herzegovina</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bulgaria" title="Bulgaria">Bulgaria</a>, <a href="/wiki/Croatia" title="Croatia">Croatia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greece</a> (usually included within Eastern Mediterranean countries)<sup id="cite_ref-Cyprus_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Cyprus-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Greece_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Greece-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Slovenia" title="Slovenia">Slovenia</a>, <a href="/wiki/North_Macedonia" title="North Macedonia">North Macedonia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Serbia" title="Serbia">Serbia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kosovo" title="Kosovo">Kosovo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Montenegro" title="Montenegro">Montenegro</a>, <a href="/wiki/Romania" title="Romania">Romania</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ukraine" title="Ukraine">Ukraine</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Eastern_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Eastern-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
