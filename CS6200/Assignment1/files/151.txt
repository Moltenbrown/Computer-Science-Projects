<p><b>Ole</b> is a Danish and Norwegian masculine given name, derived from the <a href="/wiki/Old_Norse" title="Old Norse">Old Norse</a> name <a href="/wiki/Olaf" title="Olaf">Óláfr</a>, meaning "ancestor's descendant".
</p>
