<p><b>Parker</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Surname" title="Surname">surname</a> of <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> origin, derived from <a href="/wiki/Old_French" title="Old French">Old French</a> with the meaning "keeper of the park". "Parker" was also a <a href="/wiki/Nickname" title="Nickname">nickname</a> given to <a href="/wiki/Gamekeeper" class="mw-redirect" title="Gamekeeper">gamekeepers</a> in <a href="/wiki/Medieval_England" class="mw-redirect" title="Medieval England">medieval England</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> It is the 48th-most common surname in England.<sup id="cite_ref-fb_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fb-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Within the United States, it is ranked as the 47th-most common <a href="/wiki/Surname" title="Surname">surname</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
