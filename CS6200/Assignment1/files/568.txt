	<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">American Family Day</h1>
	
	<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
		<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>
		
		
		
		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
		<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><table class="infobox vevent" style="width:22em"><tbody><tr><th colspan="2" class="hd" style="text-align:center;font-size:125%;font-weight:bold;background-color: transparent">American Family Day</th></tr><tr><th scope="row">Observed&#160;by</th><td><a href="/wiki/Arizona" title="Arizona">Arizona</a>, United States</td></tr><tr><th scope="row">Date</th><td>First Sunday in August</td></tr><tr><th scope="row">2018&#160;date</th><td>August 5   <span style="display:none">&#160;(<span class="dtstart">2018-08-05</span>)</span></td></tr><tr><th scope="row">2019&#160;date</th><td>August 4   <span style="display:none">&#160;(<span class="dtstart">2019-08-04</span>)</span></td></tr><tr><th scope="row">2020&#160;date</th><td>August 2   <span style="display:none">&#160;(<span class="dtstart">2020-08-02</span>)</span></td></tr><tr><th scope="row">2021&#160;date</th><td>August 1   <span style="display:none">&#160;(<span class="dtstart">2021-08-01</span>)</span></td></tr><tr><th scope="row">Frequency</th><td>annual</td></tr></tbody></table>
<p><b>American Family Day</b> is the 14th state-recognized holiday in Arizona, Title 1-301.<sup id="cite_ref-American_Family_Day_website_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-American_Family_Day_website-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Arizona_State_Legislature_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Arizona_State_Legislature-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>  American Family Day, much like <a href="/wiki/Mother%27s_Day" title="Mother&#39;s Day">Mothers Day</a> or <a href="/wiki/Father%27s_Day" title="Father&#39;s Day">Father's Day</a> is a non-paid holiday established as a separate day to appreciate family members by spending time with them.  Families are discouraged from buying gifts or other material items.
</p><p>Arizona resident, John Makkai, is credited with pushing the holiday through the Arizona legislation.  American Family Day began as a 1-year proclamation, signed by then Governor <a href="/wiki/Ra%C3%BAl_H%C3%A9ctor_Castro" title="Raúl Héctor Castro">Raúl Héctor Castro</a>, declaring August 7, 1977 American Family Day.  The following year, American Family Day was signed into law as an official Arizona holiday by Governor <a href="/wiki/Bruce_Babbitt" title="Bruce Babbitt">Bruce Babbitt</a>.  The holiday also caught on in several other states, including North Carolina<sup id="cite_ref-North_Carolina_Statutes_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-North_Carolina_Statutes-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and Georgia.
</p><p>From the Georgia Department of Education Parent Engagement Program,<sup id="cite_ref-Georgia_Department_of_Education_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Georgia_Department_of_Education-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> "American Family Day- this day brings families together to share their love and appreciation of one another."
</p>
